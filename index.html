This tool copies annotations from one group to another, and optionally also from one domain to another. Only top-level annotations will be copied, replies are ignored. The Hypothesis username you provide will be the creator of copied annotations. If the source group is not Public, the Hypothesis user must be a member to read annotations from it. Likewise, if the destination group is not Public, the Hypothesis user must be a member to write annotations to it.
